About Ikot Mo La Salle

Ikot Mo La Salle is an innovative map designed to help with campus navigation. It provides detailed information about each area, enhancing your experience by showcasing Lasallian heritage. This website aims to boost awareness and offer insights into the map's creation, helping students understand it better. Explore with Ikot Mo La Salle as your virtual guide, bridging you to the spirit of Lasallian education.
