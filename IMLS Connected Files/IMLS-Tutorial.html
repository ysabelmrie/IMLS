How to Utilize the Page

Learn how to navigate Ikot Mo La Salle

1. Virtual Markers of Locations in DLSU-D

The map has markers at specific locations around DLSU-D. Click on the markers to view more details about each location.

2. Get Directions

Each marker has a "Get Directions" feature. When you click on a marker and Get directions, it provides a link to Google Maps to guide you to that location.

3. Mark as Visited

Once you visit a location, you can mark it as visited. This feature helps you keep track of places you've been to.

4. Comments Feature

You can leave comments for each location. This allows you to share your thoughts or experiences with others.

5. Jazz Music Feature

By pressing the button on the top right, you can play jazz music to enjoy while browsing around the map.

6. Zoom feature

By pressing the "+" or "-" button you can either zoom or zoom out of the map.
